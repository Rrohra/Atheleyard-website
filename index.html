WHY TO CHOOSE OUR ACADEMY ?

Founded in 2013, Fencing Sport Academy aims to create an inclusive environment and a united community to train members of all ages from novice to Olympic levels.
All our coaches are Hong Kong Fencing Association (HKFA) certified. In addition to be former elite fencing athletes of the Hong Kong Team, they all possess abundant experience in coaching for many years.
All equipment used in our academy are up-to-date and compliant with HKFA CE (EN13567:2002) or equivalent standard. We also established our own code of conduct to ensure all members during trainings are safe.
Most importantly, unlike traditional trainings which often focus on sword- fighting techniques, we strive to deploy a cohesive and proactive integrated system approach to prioritize the physical, mental, and body awareness skills of our students. This not only will be of great benefit to their future development but also encourage their pursuit of personal excellence and lifelong discovery.
